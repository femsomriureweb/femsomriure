Additionally to the t-shirt project, we proposed you to collaborate on the initiative that the Vicente Ferrer Foundation carries out to raise money to buy bicycles, so that children who live far from schools can move more easily from their residences.

Initially, we were informed about the long distances and the forms of travel available in the region and we found out about the very good project that it is. And it was during our stay in Andhra Pradesh and in India in general, where we were able to see more about the ease and the good that makes them children have their own way of travel.

We received donations specifically for 20 bicycles, that is, € 1,000 in total!
