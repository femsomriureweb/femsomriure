Our objective

Make the largest number of children in India smile

We are two young Catalan currently living in Madrid who have always had in mind to carry out a solidary project. For that reason, when we were planning to travel to India next august, we saw the perfect opportunity to undertake it.

Our mission is to gather the largest number of supports to contribute covering a basic need such as clothing and, the most important, MAKE CHILDREN SMILE!

*COLOR THEIR OWN T-SHIRTS:

Necessary ingredients:

- T-shirts, brushes and paintings: With which they will have a good time coloring them and at the same time using them as clothing.

*OTHER PROJECTS WITH WHICH WE COLLABORATE WITH THE VICENTE FERRER FOUNDATION:

- Give away bicycles: To facilitate the movement of children between remote villages and the school. www.fundacionvicenteferrer.org/en/
